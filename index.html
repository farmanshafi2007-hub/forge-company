Common Questions

Everything You Need
to Know

25 questions answered clearly — in the rain 🌧️

Is this really a one-time payment with no monthly fees?+
Yes, completely. All Forge Software plans are lifetime — you pay once and the AI works forever. No subscriptions, no renewals, no hidden charges of any kind.
How does the AI get trained for my specific business?+
After purchase, contact us on WhatsApp and share your business info — products, prices, FAQs, policies. Our team trains the AI to answer exactly the way you would. Takes 1–2 working days.
What does "AI brain update" mean?+
An AI brain update means we add new knowledge — new products, new pricing, new policies, new capabilities. Basic plan gets 3–5 updates. Pro and Elite get unlimited — call us anytime.
How fast does Agent X reply to customers?+
Within 2–5 seconds of a customer's message — 24/7, 365 days a year. No delays, no breaks, no days off. Even at 3am on a Sunday.
Who is Smart Booking Software for?+
Any appointment-based business — hair salons, beauty parlors, clinics, doctors, tutors, fitness trainers, dental offices, massage centers, and more. Customers chat with the AI to book their slot.
How do I buy a plan?+
Click "Get Started" on any plan — it opens a quick setup form. Fill in your business details, and our team will contact you to confirm your order, take payment, and begin AI setup. Smooth and quick.
Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro or Elite later?+
Yes! Upgrade anytime by paying the difference. Contact us on WhatsApp — existing AI training data is preserved and we add the new features on top.
What if my business changes — can the AI be updated?+
Absolutely. Pro and Elite holders get unlimited AI brain updates. Basic plan gets 3–5. After that, upgrade or pay a small fee for additional updates.
Does Agent X really reduce fake or spam orders?+
Yes. The AI asks qualifying questions to filter out low-intent conversations. It only redirects customers to WhatsApp when they've shown genuine purchase intent — saving real time.
What languages does the AI support?+
Basic and Pro primarily support English and Hindi. The Elite plan includes multi-language covering major Indian languages so customers can interact in their preferred language.
How is Forge different from other chatbot tools?+
Most chatbots just follow a script. Forge AI understands context, evaluates buyer intent, handles objections, makes dynamic decisions — like a trained salesperson, not a FAQ bot. Plus, trained specifically on YOUR business.
Do I need any technical skills to use Forge?+
None at all. Share your business info with us on WhatsApp, and our team builds and deploys your AI. We handle all technical work — you enjoy the results.
What if the AI says something wrong?+
Message us on WhatsApp and we'll review and correct it quickly. Pro and Elite users get priority support — faster resolution times. We take accuracy seriously.
Can the AI handle multiple customers at the same time?+
Yes — unlimited simultaneous conversations. Whether 1 or 1,000 customers message at the same time, every one gets an instant personalized reply. Huge advantage over human agents.
Is my business data safe with Forge?+
Yes. Your business information is used only to train YOUR AI — never shared with other businesses or third parties. Each business gets its own private AI. Your information stays completely confidential.
How long does the initial AI setup take after I buy?+
Typically 1–2 working days. Once you share your business details over WhatsApp, our team begins training immediately. Basic setup can sometimes be done same day for simpler businesses.
Can the AI send images or product photos to customers?+
Currently the AI focuses on text-based conversations — answering questions, explaining products, booking appointments, and redirecting buyers. Image sharing capabilities are planned for future updates.
What if I have a very unique or niche business?+
No problem. Forge AI is custom-trained for each business individually. Whether you sell handmade jewelry, run a pet clinic, or offer online tutoring — we train the AI specifically for your niche.
Does the AI work on Instagram DMs or only WhatsApp?+
Currently Agent X and Smart Booking are optimized for WhatsApp, where the majority of Indian customers are most active. Instagram integration is on the roadmap for future versions.
Can I have both Agent X AND Smart Booking together?+
Yes! Many businesses use both — Agent X handles sales conversations while Smart Booking manages appointments. You can add both to your cart and we'll set them up together. Contact us for a bundle discussion.
What happens if WhatsApp goes down or has issues?+
The AI depends on WhatsApp's uptime, just like any WhatsApp-based tool. WhatsApp outages are rare and brief — Meta's infrastructure is globally reliable. During any downtime, it resumes automatically once service restores.
Can I see how many customers the AI has talked to?+
Currently basic analytics are provided on request. A full real-time AI Business Dashboard with live stats, conversation counts, and sales metrics is coming soon — join our broadcast channel to be first to know.
Is there a free trial available?+
We don't offer a standard free trial, but we do offer a live demo on WhatsApp — message us and we'll show you exactly how the AI would respond for a business like yours before you buy.
What payment methods are accepted?+
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and other common Indian payment methods. All transactions are handled directly over WhatsApp — simple, fast, and secure.
Who is Forge Software and who made it?+
Forge Software is an Indian AI company founded and built by Farman. We specialise in AI tools for small and medium Indian businesses — giving local businesses the power of smart automation at an honest, one-time price.
